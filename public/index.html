ABOUT

Most stats keeping software is very robust and hard to use for a regular team. This website was created with the idea of simplicity: simple statistical input and somewhat complex statistical output. Of course, there are many imporvements to be made to this first and simple version, but all essential are already here! Possession, passes, shots, and much more. If you have any suggestions or ideas - feel free to contact me!
